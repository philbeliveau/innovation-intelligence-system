# Regenerative Agriculture Campaign

## Description

Launch a marketing campaign highlighting Lactalis Canada’s support for regenerative farming practices, showcasing the environmental and community benefits of its partnerships with Canadian dairy farmers. This campaign will appeal to sustainability-minded consumers and organic-focused parents, reinforcing the brand’s commitment to responsible sourcing and ESG leadership.

## Actionability

- Develop campaign materials featuring stories from Canadian farmers.
- Partner with environmental organizations to certify regenerative practices.
- Promote the campaign through social media, packaging, and digital channels.
